# Environmental Volunteer Management and Impact Tracking Platform for NGOs


## Overview

This project provides a platform for managing and tracking volunteer activities for environmental initiatives.
The system enables structured monitoring of volunteer participation, attendance, and activity progress while generating insights about environmental impact through automated analysis.

The platform is designed to improve transparency, operational efficiency, and impact visibility for organizations managing community-driven environmental programs.

---

## Key Features

* **Volunteer Attendance Tracking** – Record and maintain attendance for activities and events.
* **Progress Monitoring** – Track volunteer participation and activity completion over time.
* **Impact Mapping** – Generate structured insights about environmental initiatives using NLP-based analysis.
* **Centralized Data Management** – Maintain organized records of volunteers, events, and participation.
* **Scalable System Design** – Designed to support expansion for larger volunteer communities.

---

## System Capabilities

* Structured data tracking for volunteer engagement
* Event and participation monitoring
* Environmental initiative impact analysis
* Centralized record management
